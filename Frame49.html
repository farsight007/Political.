GDP & FDI
India's GDP is projected to grow between 6.5% to 7% in 2024-25. The economy showed resilience with an 8.2% growth in FY 2024, exceeding 8% in three out of four quarters. India ranks as the fifth-largest economy globally in 2024, with a GDP of $3.94 trillion. The services sector remains the largest contributor to GDP at 54.7%, followed by industry at 27.6% and agriculture at 17.7%. India continues to attract significant foreign direct investment (FDI), with inflows expected to increase due to government initiatives to liberalize FDI policies and improve ease of doing business.
